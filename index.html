Workshop on Dynamical Systems and Mathematical Physics

The Workshop aims to gather several experts in the field of dynamical systems and mathematical physics, providing them an opportunity to interact, discuss classical problems, explore emerging trends and facilitate future collaborations.

The Workshop takes place 2025 December 15-19 in Great Bay University, located at Songshan Lake Park, Dongguan, Guangdong, China. Participants are expected to arrive on Sunday December 14, and depart on Saturday December 20. We look forward to your participation in a mutually stimulating and productive environment at the Workshop and Great Bay University.

Organizers
