Logistics

All the talks are in Room 206 in the Teaching Building on campus, Great Bay University/大湾区大学 (address: Da Xue Lu 16, Song Shan Hu, Dongguan, Guangdong, China/东莞市松山湖大学路16号).

We have reserved rooms for all parcipants at Songhu Yingbinli Hotel/松湖迎宾里 (address: Boheng Er Lu 1 Hao, Song Shan Hu, Dongguan, Guangdong, China/东莞市松山湖博恒二路1号).

The hotel is about 2km from campus (10 mins by car, 25 mins by walk).

Transportation

You may reach Dongguan by flight or high-speed rail. We will have volunteers at Shenzhen airport, Guangzhou airport and Songshan Lake Terminal to help with communications/translations.

By Rail/高铁

There are several high-speed rail stations nearby and the most covenient options are Humen Railway Station/虎门站, Dongguannan Railway Station/东莞南站 and Shenzhenbei Railway Station/深圳北站.

There are no direct connections by bus/metro from the rail stations to the hotel/campus and we recommend taking a taxi (of course you are welcome to adventure with the public transport if you want).

By Air/飞机

There are three major airports nearby:

  1. Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport/深圳宝安机场
  2. Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport/广州白云机场
  3. Hong Kong International Airport/香港国际机场

Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport/深圳宝安机场 is the closest (1h ride). Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport/广州白云机场 (1h 45min ride) and Hong Kong International Airport/香港国际机场 have more international flights.

  • From Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport/深圳宝安机场
    After arriving at Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport:
    • You can get a ride to hotel/campus by taxi at Exit Gate 13 and by e-ride hailing service at Exit Gate 15. (recommended)
    • You can also take a bus at Exit Gate 17 on the second floor to Songshan Lake Terminal, and then take a taxi.
    • You can also go to B1 floor and take the subway to Bitou Station/碧头站 at the border of Shenzhen/Dongguan, then take a taxi (not recommended).
  • From Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport/广州白云机场
    After arriving at Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport:
    • You can take a taxi directly to hotel/campus and it is about 1h 45 mins. (recommended)
    • You can go to Terminal 1, Area A: Outside gates A3-A5 (self-service ticket machines inside gates A4 and A10), take a bus to Songshan Lake Terminal, and then take a taxi.
    • You can take a bus outside Gate B10-B13 of Terminal 1 (self-service ticket machines inside Gate B5 and B12) to Songshan Lake Terminal, and then take a taxi.
    • You can go to T2 Terminal: Transportation Center West Bus Station (self-service ticket machine for boarding), take a bus to Songshan Lake Terminal, and then take a taxi.
  • From Hong Kong International Airport/香港国际机场
    After arriving at Hong Kong International Airport:
    • You can go to the Hong Kong Bus airport ticket counter: T1 terminal, L5 floor lobby, exit A, turn left to the MO2 counter, inquire about the bus to Songshan Lake, then purchase a ticket and take the bus to Songshan Lake terminal, and then take a taxi to campus/hotel.
    • You can go to the ticket counters at the (Hong Kong Business Bus) airport: T1 terminal A17-18 and MO4 counters, inquire about the bus to Songshan Lake, then purchase tickets and take the bus to Songshan Lake terminal, and then take a taxi.

Practical Info

The December weather in Dongguan is roughly 10 - 18 degrees in Celsius, not much rain. You may check the most up-to-date weather info before travel and pack accordingly.

It is strongly recommended that you have Chinese electronic payment Apps (such as Alipay or Wechat Pay) ready on your phone, as people in China nowadays seldom use cash and you may run into difficulty getting change if you pay big buck in cash. For more info you may check out China Travel Guide.
